About

Greetings!

I am a computer scientist, artificial intelligence researcher, amateur blogger and music enthusiast. I enjoy food, travel and learning about different cultures and their mythology.

My research focuses on machine learning and deep learning methods and their utility in solving applied Natural Language Processing problems. I recently earned my Masters in Computer Science from University of Massachusetts Amherst (Go UMASS!!), and am now working at AlphaSense Oy, where I research and employ NLP solutions to assist their AI powered search engine.

I borrow my life philosophy from Einstein : If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough. I wish to demystify AI and make it accessible to people across domains and soon plan to start a series of blogs with this in mind.

I always look forward to working on challenging and meaningful machine learning problems that not only stretch the human imagination and creativity but are also applicable in the real world, making everyday lives easier. I am also looking to connect with people who share my enthusiasm for AI, so feel free to contact me via Linkedin.
